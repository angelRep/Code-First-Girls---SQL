Cyprus

Cyprus lies about 40 miles (65 km) south of Turkey, 60 miles (100 km) west of Syria, and 480 miles (770 km) southeast of mainland Greece.

Cyprus is famous for

  1. The Limassol carnival
  2. Haloumi cheese
  3. Commandaria, the sweet dessert wine from Cyprus
  4. Palouzes & shoushoukos***
  5. Many more...
